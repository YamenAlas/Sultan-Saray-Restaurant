About Sultan Saray

Welcome to Sultan Saray, where the rich history of Egypt meets the authenticity of flavor and tradition. Since our establishment in the heart of Cairo, we take pride in offering a unique dining experience that combines warm hospitality with delectable dishes inspired by both Eastern and international cuisines. Our vision is to be the top destination for food lovers in Egypt by consistently delivering high-quality service and exceptional dining experiences.
